Indian chess player, gold medalist and social media personality. In 2013, he received the title of Grandmaster at age 19, which is the highest title a chess player can be awarded. He became only the 30th chess player from India to be awarded the Grandmaster title.

He attended school at the Fravashi Academy, where he learned how to play chess. At age 14, he won the U-14 division at the World Youth Chess Championship, becoming the first Indian chess player to do so.

During the 2020 FIDE Online Chess Olympiad, he won a gold medal while serving as captain of the Indian National Chess team.

He was born in Indore, India, where he lived with his parents, Santosh Gujrathi and Nikita Santosh Gujrathi, as well as his younger sister named Vedika.

He was born the same year as the international chess prodigy Anish Giri.

Monica Calhoun Net Worth

October 05, 2024

Monica Calhoun has built an estimated net worth of $7 Million throughout her acting career. She was born on 29 July 1971 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. Calhoun is best known for her roles in movies such as “Bagdad Cafe”, “The Players Club”, “The Salon”, “The Best Man” and its sequel “The Best Man Holiday”.

As a child, Calhoun moved to Los Angeles, California, where she began her acting career, making her film debut in the 1985 biopic “Children Of The Night”. She gained recognition for her role in the German comedy film “Bagdad Cafe” in 1987. Her versatile acting skills led her to diverse roles in both television and film.

In 1998, Calhoun starred in the hit movie “The Players Club” as a stripper Ebony and the next year saw her role in the comedy/drama film “The Best Man” as bride-to-be Mia Morgan, which earned her a nomination for NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Actress in a Motion Picture.

Over the years, she has been seen in various movies such as “Love and Basketball”, “Trois 2: Pandora’s Box”, “Gang of Roses”, “Civil Brand” and more, alongside guest appearances in popular television shows including “Grey’s Anatomy”, “Everybody Hates Chris” and “Cold Case”.

Calhoun is well-recognized in the television and film industry, particularly in Hollywood’s African-American community. Her career also expanded to web series, where she portrayed the leading role in “Diary of a Single Mom”. She reprised her role as Mia in the 2013 sequel “The Best Man Holiday”, further solidifying her standing in the industry and improving her financial standing.

Sources indicate that despite her successful career, Calhoun has remained single and is known to have a son with unspecified special needs.

Brittany Rainey Fox 13

Brittany currently works at FOX 13 in Tampa, Florida as a Meteorologist. Prior to joining FOX 13 in January 2020, she worked at FOX 59 & CBS4 networks in Indianapolis, indiana as a Meteorologist. She joined FOX 59 in November 2015, and while working there she got the chance to witness the one-hundredth Indy 500. She was a member of ‘Storm Meteorologist’ team as well as appearing on the ‘Work For You’ Show.

Rainey spent three years working at KJRH-TV Channel 2 in Tulsa, Oklahoma as a Meteorologist. Some of the activities she performed include the driving of the Lead Car in the Mid-America Ford Shelby Nationals, assisting alongside the Philbrook Museum’s Festivals of Trees and portraying a Walk on Role at the ‘Shakespeare in the Park.’ She also worked at Hoak Media of Nebraska, KHAS-TV Channel 5, an NBC affiliate in Huntington, Nebraska.

Brittany began working for the station on the weekend shows after which she was promoted to weekday evening shows. She spent two years at KHAS News 5, and later relocated to Huntington, West Virginia from where she focused on weather casting for four different regions each weekend. She then moved to Tulsa, Oklahoma working for Channel 2 and covered tornados at large for about three years (2012-2015).

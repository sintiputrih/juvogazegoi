Birth Facts and Education

Regarding Urvashi’s educational background and qualifications, she attended DAV School in Kotdwar and Gargi College in New Delhi. Later on, she attended Hindu College and the University of Delhi for her higher studies.

Career and Professional Life

Urvashi Rautela first planned to become an engineer and even appeared for IIT exams but due to her participation in beauty contests, she got inclined towards the fashion industry. And, she got her breakthrough when she was 15 years old in Wills Lifestyle India Fashion Week.

She also was able to win the title of “Miss Teen India” in the year. Urvashi was able to become the show stopper as a teen model for Lakme Fashion Week and got the opportunity to walk the ramp at Amazon Fashion Week, Bombay Fashion Week, and Dubai Fashion Week.

Likewise, Urvashi Rautela was able to win “Indian Princess”, “Miss Tourism World”, as well as “Miss Asian Supermodel”, all of them in the year 2011. In the same year, she also was able to win the title of “Miss Tourism Queen Of The Year 2011” held in China.

Through this winning, Rautela was able to become the first Indian woman to win the pageant. Moreover, after her winning, she was offered Ishaqzaade but turned it down as she wanted to focus on the Miss Universe pageant.

Rautela was able to win the coveted crown of “I AM She – Miss Universe India” in the year 2012 and also the special award for “Miss Photogenic”. Unfortunately, she was disqualified and had to relinquish her crown as she was underage at that time.

However, in the year 2015, she again joined the Indian pageantry and then won the title. After that, she was able to represent India in “Miss Universe 2015”, but did not win.

More About Career

Urvashi Rautela made her debut in the Bollywood industry with the film “Singh Saab The Great” as the female lead. She then appeared in Yo Yo Honey Singh’s international video album “Love Dose” which was a hit.

Soon after, she began working in the South Indian film industry and made her debut with the Kannada film “Mr. Airavata”. She was praised for her dancing throughout this movie.

Likewise, Urvashi then appeared in two other Hindi films, “Sanam Re” and “Great Grand Masti” as well as two music videos in the year 2016.

Moreover, in the year 2017, she starred in a special dance sequence ‘Haseeno Ka Deewana’ in the film “Kaabil” and made a special appearance in the Bangladeshi film “Porobashinee”. She also starred in the revenge drama “Hate Story 4” in the year 2018.

After that, she appeared in Anees Bazmee’s comedy film “Pagalpanti” in February 2019. Then in the year 2020, Rautela starred in “Virgin Bhanupriya” which is a comedy-drama.

Furthermore, Urvashi Rautela even launched an app that gave information about her to her fans. The government and the tourism of Andaman & Nicobar Islands named her the “Youngest Most Beautiful Woman In The Universe” in the year 2018.

She also was able to win the “Uttrakhand Maharatna Award” from the chief minister of Uttrakhand. Moreover, Rautela even became the first Indian woman to walk the runway of the Emirati label Amato in the year 2020.

This runaway was showcased on the official calendar of the Arab Fashion Week, an event endorsed by the Dubai-based Arab Fashion Council.

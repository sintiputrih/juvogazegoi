Topshop Floral Jacquard-Dot Long-Sleeve Dress

This new Topshop Floral Jacquard-Dot Long-Sleeve Dress would make for a great winter-to-spring dress to have on hand. Its maxi length and long sleeves make it great for keeping you warm, and the pink and red floral print give it a spring-ready vibe. I would pair it with knee-high boots or matching open-toe kitten heels and a leather jacket. It comes in sizes 2 through 14 and features a lightweight spotted jacquard fabric, a high neckline, waist ruching and long, balloon sleeves with puffed shoulders and elastic cuffs. Additionally, the dress has a relaxed fit and a button keyhole closure for easy on and off.

Mango Denim Midi Skirt

If you have been in the market for a classic denim skirt, look no further than this Denim Midi Skirt from Mango. Available in sizes XS to XL, it’s made from a non-stretch, 100 percent cotton denim and comes in a dark blue wash. The skirt has a straight-cut silhouette with a high-waisted design, a front slit so it’s easy to walk in, and five functional pockets between the front and back. I’d pair this with a cozy oversized sweater and fitted knee-high boots.

Glamnetic Short Almond Press-On Nails Set

I stopped going to the nail salon and made the switch over to press-on nails over two years ago so I could save money, and I’ll never go back. My go-to brand is Glamnetic, so when I came across the Short Almond Press-On Nails Set in the Nordstrom’s new arrivals section, it was an immediate add to cart. The set comes with 30 nails in 15 sizes for a custom fit, and you can snag it in three different options: a two-toned French tip, multicolored French tip, and my personal favorite, pink aura design. The nails have a semi-transparent base, and they include a cuticle stick, nail glue, nail file, and an alcohol pad.

ODESZA To Headline NYE After Hours Warehouse Party In Brooklyn

May 16, 2024
blog

Recommended Videos

If you’re the kind of partygoer who would prefer to listen to real music while out and about this New Years Eve and will be in or around Brooklyn, New York, then you owe it to yourself to catch ODESZA at Inception New Years Eve 2016. The Seattle-based duo will be performing a set at the after hours event in an as-yet-undisclosed warehouse location, making for a proper alternative for anyone looking to keep the party going into the wee hours.

ODESZA’s rise in popularity over the past few years has been a favorite story of many in the electronic music industry. Not only did their music gain popularity through largely organic methods – they also perform it almost entirely on the spot with live controllers, often times jamming out new riffs and updated melodies in the moment as few electronic music artists do. The duo will be joined by supporting talent Julia Niko and Chrome Sparks, the latter of whom will perform a DJ set as opposed to a live set.

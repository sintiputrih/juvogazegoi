Kaufman Career

Kaufman has studied and worked extensively as a professional actor in films and television since he started his career in 1982. He has worked with Tim Daly on Superman: The Animated Series and its subsequent spin-offs, playing the role of  Jimmy Olsen.  in 1995, he together with his wife, Lisa Picotte appeared with Daly in the series Wings, the two represented a couple whose wedding Daly’s character Joe and his fiancée Helen crash.

Kaufman has also worked as a commercial actor. One of his roles was as a dancing stockboy in a 1989 commercial for Hi-C Ecto Cooler.

Biography

Gabrielle Bell was born in England on March 24, 1976. After rising to fame for her Lucky series of autobiographical cartoons, Bell published a cartoon collection titled Cecil and Jordan in New York. She also frequently contributed to journals such as Stereoscomic, Orchid, and Shout! She collaborated with film director Michel Gondry on a cinematic adaptation of one of her Cecil and Jordan in New York cartoons. After taking classes at both Humboldt State University and the City College of San Francisco, she worked in retail until she was able to establish herself as an artist.

Kris Kristofferson Parents: Meet Lars Henry Kristofferson And Mary Ann Ashbrook

Lars Henry Kristofferson and Mary Ann Ashbrook are the parents of Kris Kristofferson.

Kris’s father Lars Henry Kristofferson was born on August 16, 1905, in Tacoma, Pierce County, Washington.

He died from a Heart attack in Fallbrook, San Diego County, California on January 01, 1971.

Lars Henry Kristofferson was 65 at the time of his death.

Kris’ mother Mary Ann Ashbrook was born on September 8, 1911, in Columbus, Franklin, Ohio, United States to Roy Wilson Ashbrook and Anna Louise Adams.

Mary Ann Ashbrook married Lars Henry Kristofferson. She passed away on February 2, 1985, in Fallbrook, San Diego, California, USA.

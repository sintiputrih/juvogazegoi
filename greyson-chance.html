Greyson Chance is an American singer, songwriter, and musician. It was in the year 2010 that he shot to popularity after his performance of Lady Gaga’s “Paparazzi” at a grade school music festival went viral on YouTube, gaining over 70 million views. Since then, he has released many tracks, albums, and extended plays such as Hold On ’til the Night, Portraits, Somewhere Over My Head, Trophies, Waiting Outside the Lines, Dancing Next to Me, Yours, Boots, Sunshine & City Lights, Afterlife, Good as Gold, Take a Look at Me Now, Thrilla In Manila, Twenty One, and Low.

Otar Saralidze As Domenico On Netflix’s 365 Days

Otar Saralidze portrayed “Domenico,” Massimo’s associate on 365 Days: This Day. His character and Olga (Magdalena Lamparska) later develop feelings for each other and ….. let’s not spoil the rest.

However, we do like to mention Domenico wasn’t treated very kindly by Massimo (Michele Morrone). And we’re not the only one who feels this way. “I’m not calling Domenico a bitch but why tf you letting Massimo snatch you up like a pit bull puppy,” a fan tweeted.

Is Otar Saralidze Gay?

No, Otar Saralidze isn’t gay.

Likewise, his co-stars from 365 Days: This Day, Michele Morrone, and Simone Susinna aren’t gay either. Though Michele shared an evocative pool selfie with  Simone in his arms, he later took to his Stories again to clarify that he isn’t gay!

As per the actor, Simone and he became very good friends, and they took the selfie as brothers. “Guys, it was just a picture. Nothing more,” he emphasized.

But with this being said, Michele confessed that he was a “very big supporter” of the LGBT community.

Also, Michele justified the caption he wrote on the photo, which read, “I’m a liar.” — “An actor is a kind of liar. That’s why I wrote that. Acting…someone who is not. I didn’t come out.”

Does Otar Saralidze Have A Wife?

No, Otar Saralidze didn’t have a wife as of 2022. The wedding pictures you’ve probably seen over his and Anna Maria Sieklucka’s IG aren’t real. Reportedly, the ceremony was just a rehearsal for 365 Days: This Day.

In case you missed it, it was June 16, 2020, when Otar first uploaded his and Anna’s wedding pictures over his FB story captioned, “Sorry Massimo”. Naturally, fans quickly were to conclude that the stars got married.

However, a day later, Anna then took it to her IG to share the same pictures with a long list of credits to the organizers. So, it didn’t take fans long to realize the pictures were from the rehearsals

Talking about Otar’s co-star, Anna-Maria played “Laura Biel” in 365 Days (2020) and 365 Days: This Day (2022). Her character Laura and Massimo (Michele Morrone) get married on 365 Days: This Day.

But Laura and Michele aren’t the on-screen lovers. The two also brought their on-screen chemistry out into the real world and were rumored to be dating. The last we saw them, the duo was spotted exploring Milan, hand in hand. (Tramadol) Also, on the premiere of 365 Days: This Day, they didn’t shy away from showing affection.

Prior to this, Laura was in a relationship with director Łukasz Witt-Michałowski.

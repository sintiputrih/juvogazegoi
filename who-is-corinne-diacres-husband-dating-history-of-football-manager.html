Corinne Diacre Professional Career

Corinne Diacre played as a defender at Soyaux in Division 1 and in the France national team.

Diacre is one of the rare one-club footballers who has spent the entirety of her playing career with Soyaux, where she played for almost two decades from 1988 to 2007. She played around 350 D1 matches for Soyaux in her club career, which was a record at that time.

The 47-year-old represented her country in international games with 121 appearances and 14 goals. She debuted for the French national team on March 9, 1993, at the age of 18. 

The French former footballer's playing career was cut short a the age of 32 due to an ACL tear in a matchup against Montpellier in October 2006.

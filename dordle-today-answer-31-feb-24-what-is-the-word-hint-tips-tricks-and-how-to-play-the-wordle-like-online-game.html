Here’s the answer to today Dordle words #31 (published on February 24th, 2022), as well as some hints

Dordle is a more difficult variant of Wordle in that you must guess two words at the same time, and you only get seven chances to guess both Dordle words. Each guess you make counts as a guess for both words, so you must choose your words carefully. Every day at midnight in your time zone, a new puzzle is issued, which is the same time as Wordle.

Dordle Words Hints Today

Here are some hints to assist you in completing Dordle 31.
Hint 1: The letter I is the common letter between both Dordle words today.
Hint 2: There is one vowel in the first word and three in the second.
Hint 3: The first word begins with a T and the second on begins with an E.
Hint 4: The first word is a part of your body and the second word is to be banished from somewhere.

What Is The Dordle 31 Answer Today Of The Wordle Inspired Game? (Feb 24, 2022)

Here’s how I came up with the answers to today’s Dordle 31 problem. I originally guessed the word ‘URINE,’ which proved to be a good guess because it correctly placed the I in the first word and the I and E in the second. Then I decided to make a guess at the term ‘STILE.’ This revealed that the first word contained a T and also placed the L in the proper position for the second word.

I then decided to concentrate on the second word, so I chose the word ‘WHILE.’ Unfortunately, this was not the proper solution for the second word, but it did get the H in the right place for the first. Because I couldn’t think of any words that ended in ILE but didn’t have an H, I decided to concentrate on the first word. I chose the word ‘THICK,’ which put the T in the correct spot. Then I made my second guess, which was the answer to Dordle 31’s first word…

THIGH!

I still didn’t know any of the first letters for the second word, so I guessed ‘MAILE,’ even though I hadn’t used the A yet. Unfortunately, this was not the correct answer, because the term did not contain an A. It took me a while, but after looking at the letters left, I eventually guessed the second word, which is…

EXILE!

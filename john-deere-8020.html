The John Deere 8020 was introduced after troubles occurred with the John Deere 8010. The John Deere 8020s were model 8010 tractors rebuilt with a new transmission.

John Deere 8020 Four-wheel Drive (4WD) Tractor was equipped with a Detroit Diesel 671E diesel 6-cylinder liquid-cooled 425 ci [7.0 L] engine, which delivers 215 hp [160.3 kW] power at rated rpm 2100.

John Deere 8020 Four-wheel Drive (4WD) Tractor had a Detroit Diesel 7.0L 6-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 articulated 4WD, articulated power steering, 19700 lbs [8935 kg] weight, and 8 forward and 2 reverse gears, and electric starter.

Moonshiners Star Jim Tom Hedrick Obituary And Tribute

The well-known Jim Tom Hedrick, who starred in the Discovery Channel series “Moonshiners,” passed away in 2023 at the age of 82. Both moonshiners and viewers of the popular television program mourned the passing of him as the end of an era.

Jim, who was born on Christmas Day in 1940, spent a large portion of his life honing his craft of moonshining and eventually rose to legendary status in the Appalachian region. His entry into the world of moonshining began when he was a young boy, and his skills and knowledge of the trade improved with time.

After joining the cast of “Moonshiners” in 2012, a documentary that followed the lives of moonshiners as they operated their covert distilleries throughout the Appalachian Mountains, Jim Tom gained notoriety. Millions of people were quickly won over by his upbeat and amiable personality.

Jim Tom became a well-known figure on the program because viewers came in to hear his entertaining stories and anecdotes in addition to watching the precise moonshining procedure.

Apart from his appearance on television, Jim was well-known for his expertise in the moonshining industry. He had an impact on Sugarlands Distilling Company as well, helping to establish the brand’s credibility. The distillery valued Jim Tom’s endorsement greatly since it demonstrated his status as a true authority on moonshining.

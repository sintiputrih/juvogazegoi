Jared Freid - Age, Family, Bio

February 06, 2024
blog

Comedian who has gained fame through both his standup comedy and the two podcasts that he hosts. In November of 2021, he was a guest on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. On the show, he performed a set talking about the differences between college kids and adults. He was named one of JFL's New Faces in 2017. He released his first full-length comedy album, Always a Momma Bear, in 2019. 

Before Fame

Before becoming a standup, he sold life insurance and annuities. He performed his first comedy set on April 1st, 2010. 

Trivia

He hosts the JTrain Podcast, which has gained 450,000 monthly listeners. He co-hosts U Up? with Jordana Abraham, which has amassed 750,000 monthly listeners. He landed a deal hosting a show called Dating Makeover, a weekly Spotify Greenroom show. 

Family Life

His father is from Newton, Massachusetts. His mother is from Detroit, Michigan. 

Associated With

Pete Davidson, Michael Che, Bert Kreischer, and Dave Attell are a few of the notable guests he has had on his podcast.

Wes Hampton (born December 8, 1977) is a tenor who sings with the Gaither Vocal Band as the second tenor. He became a member of the group in 2005, after the departure of David Phelps. He formerly sung as the group’s first tenor from 2005 to 2009, and he is currently a member of the current lineup, which includes Bill Gaither on bass, Adam Crabb on lead, Todd Suttles on baritone, Phelps on first tenor, and Hampton on tenor (2nd tenor). He also does solo performances from time to time.

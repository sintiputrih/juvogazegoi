Name:Brad Goreski

Hometown:Port Perry, Ontario, CN

About

Fans first fell in love with the ever-stylish Brad Goreski when he appeared on Bravo’s reality series The Rachel Zoe Project. Born in Port Perry, Ontario, Goreski moved to the United States to pursue his love of fashion.

As a student at the University of Southern California, Goreski interned in for Vogue and W magazines. After graduating with a B.A. in Art History, Goreski was hired as Vogue‘s West Coast assistant.

In 2008, Goreski left Vogue to work for Rachel Zoe. During that time, he appeared on The Rachel Zoe Project for three seasons. Though he was eventually promoted to Style Director for Rachel Zoe Inc., he left the company in 2011.

In January 2012, Goreski’s spinoff series, It’s a Brad, Brad World, premiered on Bravo. It focuses on Goreski’s life as a celebrity stylist and his relationship with longtime love Gary Janetti.

The Reason the Falcons Need to Trade Kyle Pitts

February 07, 2024
blog

Kyle Pitts, a highly-touted tight end out of the University of Florida, has been a pivotal figure for the Atlanta Falcons since being drafted 4th overall in the 2021 NFL Draft. However, despite his undeniable talent, there are compelling reasons why the Falcons should consider trading him to secure future assets and provide him with a fresh start.

Kyle Pitts: A Brief Introduction

Born on October 6, 2000, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Pitts quickly rose to prominence during his college years at the University of Florida. Known for his exceptional athleticism and receiving ability, he became a top prospect in the 2021 NFL Draft.

Performance and Stats

Pitts' performance in college and his subsequent draft selection underscored his potential as a game-changing tight end. In his rookie season with the Falcons, Pitts exhibited flashes of brilliance, amassing 68 receptions for 1,026 yards and 5 touchdowns. His remarkable numbers and impact on the field earned him recognition as one of the most promising young talents in the league.

The Disappointment and Lack of Support

Despite Pitts' undeniable talent, his tenure with the Falcons has been marred by disappointment. While he has shown flashes of brilliance, the lack of consistent and high-quality quarterback play has hindered his ability to fully showcase his skills. Playing with quarterbacks at the twilight of their careers, such as Matt Ryan, and inexperienced ones like Desmond Ridder and Taylor Heinicke, has undoubtedly impacted Pitts' development.

Moreover, the Falcons' subpar game planning and overall team performance have also contributed to Pitts' struggles. These factors have collectively stymied his growth and prevented him from reaching his full potential in Atlanta.

The Case for Trading Kyle Pitts

Given the challenges Pitts has faced in Atlanta, there is a compelling argument for the Falcons to trade him. Here's why:

  • Acquiring Future Assets: Trading Pitts could yield valuable draft picks and players that could significantly bolster the Falcons' roster in the long term. This strategic move would provide the team with the resources needed to build a competitive and sustainable future.

  • Fresh Start for Pitts: A change of scenery could be exactly what Pitts needs to thrive in a new environment. Moving to a team with a stable quarterback situation and a well-defined offensive strategy could unlock his full potential and allow him to flourish as a premier tight end in the league.

  • Financial Flexibility: By trading Pitts before the need for a new contract extension arises, the Falcons can free up financial resources to address other crucial areas of their roster, thereby enhancing the overall competitiveness of the team.

In the End

In conclusion, while Kyle Pitts undeniably possesses exceptional talent, his time with the Falcons has been marked by challenges that have impeded his growth and impact. By trading him, the Falcons can secure valuable assets for the future and provide Pitts with an opportunity to thrive elsewhere. Ultimately, a trade could serve as a win-win scenario, benefiting both the Falcons and Kyle Pitts himself.

As the NFL landscape continues to evolve, the decision to trade Kyle Pitts could shape the future of the Atlanta Falcons and the career trajectory of this immensely talented young player.

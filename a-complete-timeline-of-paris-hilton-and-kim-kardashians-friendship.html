A Complete Timeline Of Paris Hilton And Kim Kardashian's Friendship

July 24, 2024
blog

Things took a rocky turn for Paris Hilton beginning in September 2006. She was arrested after leaving In-N-Out Burger "on suspicion of DUI" and, in addition to fines, "was sentenced to three years' probation," per E! However, in February 2007, Hilton was pulled over for speeding and problems arose when it became apparent that she was "driving with a suspended license." As a result, Hilton "was sentenced to 45 days in jail" at the Century Regional Detention Facility in the city of Lynwood, in Los Angeles County.

In response to the news, Kim Kardashian offered her support and when praised by Hollywood.TV for standing by her friend, she responded with ease. "Of course," she said, "You have to. You have to support people that you love." But their friendship cooled at this point. In her post-jail interview with Larry King on CNN, Hilton cryptically said: "I've gotten rid of a lot of people ... I don't want to hurt anyone's feelings, but they know who they are."

In the midst of all of this, Kardashian's sex tape got leaked and it seemed like the perfect chance to get some guidance from Hilton, but this didn't happen. "I don't think she was that happy," Kardashian told Rolling Stone (via HuffPost). "We didn't really talk about it. I probably would have thought, 'Oh, my gosh, let me give her advice,' but we had no communication." Kardashian added, "But our friendship had fizzled before that."

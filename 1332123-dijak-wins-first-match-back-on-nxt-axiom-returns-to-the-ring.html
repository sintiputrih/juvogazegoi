Dijak Wins First Match Back On NXT

Get ready to feast your eyes on some  “hard justice” in WWE NXT.

On the November 29 episode of NXT, returned to the ring and competed on his first match on the brand since 2020. He had been a member of the main roster since his call-up as a member of Retribution in 2020. Dijak made his return to NXT on the November 21 episode and attacked NXT North American Champion Wes Lee. The following week, the powerhouse picked up an impressive win over Dante Chen.

After the bell, Dijak made it clear that he came back to NXT to dish out hard justice and pain. He named Bron Breakker and a number of other stars, as they were all seemingly on his list.

Axiom Returns To The Ring

Like Dijak, Axiom returned to the ring on Tuesday night. He had been sidelined since the November 8 episode of NXT, when he faced JD McDonagh in a singles match. McDonagh bent Axiom’s leg until it popped, so the high-flyer was forced to miss a few weeks. However, he confronted Javier Bernal on Tuesday night and told him that he was cleared to compete. He then stated that he’d get a match between the two approved, and it was confirmed for later in the show.

Once it was time for the match, Axiom scored the hard-fought win.

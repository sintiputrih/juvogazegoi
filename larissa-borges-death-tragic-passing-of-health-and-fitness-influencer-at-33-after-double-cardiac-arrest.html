Larissa Borges death: Tragic passing of health and fitness influencer at 33 after double cardiac arr

The digital realm mourns the untimely demise of Larissa Borges, a 33-year-old Instagram luminary renowned for her insights into health, fitness, and globe-trotting adventures. On August 28, 2023, Larissa’s vibrant life came to a tragic end due to a double cardiac arrest, leaving her legion of followers in shock and sorrow.

Larissa’s journey took a devastating turn on August 20 when she suffered a cardiac arrest while in Gramado, leading to her hospitalization. However, despite efforts to restore her well-being, she faced another cardiac arrest on August 28, a day that would forever mark her departure.

While the exact cause of Larissa’s passing remains undisclosed, preliminary findings from a New York Post report suggest a potential link to intoxication. Deputy Gustavo Barcellos has initiated an investigation into her passing, revealing that she had reportedly consumed various substances, including alcohol.

Barcellos shared insights into the ongoing investigation, stating, “The body has undergone necropsy, and we are conducting laboratory tests to ascertain the substances she may have ingested.”

Larissa Borges garnered a significant online following, boasting around 30,000 Instagram devotees who admired her dedication to health, fitness, and globetrotting. Her captivating posts offered a window into her ventures across the world and her commitment to physical well-being.

In her posts, she not only shared picturesque scenes from her travels to locales like Fernando de Noronha and Florianopolis but also championed the significance of physical education and exercise. Her fitness regimen and energetic personality struck a chord with her fans, earning her a loyal and appreciative audience.

Days before her passing, Larissa shared a poignant collection of images on her Instagram, accompanied by the words, “God gave us life, and with him life is eternal.”

Her family conveyed the heartbreaking news of her departure via her Instagram account on August 29, 2023, leaving her followers devastated. Translated into English, the statement read:

“The anguish of losing someone so young, at just 33, is overwhelming. Our hearts are shattered, and the emptiness we will experience is beyond words.”

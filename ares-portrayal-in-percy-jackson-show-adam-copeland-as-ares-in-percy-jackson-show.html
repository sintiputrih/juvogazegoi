About Percy Jackson Show

The Percy Jackson Show is a captivating fantasy TV series available on Disney+. This series is an adaptation of the immensely popular Percy Jackson & the Olympians books written by Rick Riordan. The storyline revolves around the adventures of Percy Jackson, a 12-year-old demigod who is falsely accused of stealing Zeus’s lightning bolt. The main cast includes Walker Scobell as Percy, Leah Sava Jeffries as Annabeth, and Aryan Simhadri as Grover. The show stays faithful to the original books, dedicating each season to one book installment.

The series features various iconic characters such as Sally Jackson, Chiron, Dionysus, and Ares, all portrayed by different actors. Filming for the series took place in Vancouver and utilized cutting-edge technology for visual effects. The music for the show is composed by Bear McCreary. It premiered on December 19, 2023, and garnered positive reviews for its faithfulness to the source material and engaging storytelling.

Adam Copeland as Ares in Percy Jackson Show

In the Disney+ Percy Jackson series, the role of Ares is portrayed by Adam Copeland, also known as Edge during his time in WWE. Adam Copeland brings a perfect blend of menace and humor to the character, making Ares a captivating presence in the world of Percy Jackson. Copeland’s transition from wrestling to acting is evident in his performance, adding depth to the character and making Ares a noteworthy and entertaining part of the series. Known for his WWE career as Edge, Copeland has successfully ventured into acting, having previously appeared in shows like Vikings.

Percy Jackson Show: The Plot

The series follows the enthralling escapades of Percy Jackson, a 12-year-old demigod wrongly accused of stealing Zeus’s lightning bolt. In this Disney+ series, Percy, played by Walker Scobell, embarks on a quest with his friends Annabeth (Leah Sava Jeffries) and Grover (Aryan Simhadri) to clear his name and restore order to Olympus. The storyline unfolds with Percy discovering his divine powers and confronting challenges from both gods and monsters.

The trio encounters various mythical beings, including Ares, the god of war, played by Adam Copeland. The show delves into Percy’s journey to Camp Half-Blood, encounters with mythological creatures, and the looming threat of war among the gods. The first season, comprising eight episodes, seamlessly blends faithful adaptation with captivating storytelling, earning positive reviews for its portrayal of Riordan’s beloved characters and the enchanting world he created.

Ares in Percy Jackson Show

In the Percy Jackson series, Ares, the God of War in Greek mythology and one of the Twelve Olympians, is portrayed by Adam Copeland, also known as Edge from WWE. The character is depicted as the much older cousin of Percy Jackson, being a son of Zeus and Hera. Ares exhibits a strong affinity for violence and war, overseeing historical conflicts and anticipating a future war among the gods.

His relationships with other gods, such as a past romance with Aphrodite and rivalry with Athena, add complexity to his character in the show. In Episode 5, Ares, played by Copeland, emerges as a significant obstacle for Percy and his friends with his menacing yet humorous portrayal.

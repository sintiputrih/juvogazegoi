James Michael Taylor Wikipedia

American actor James Michael Tyler was born. His portrayal of “Gunther” on the top-rated American sitcom “Friends” made him well-known.

James Michael Tyler portrayed Gunther on the sitcom Friends from 1994 until 2004. Gunther, a Central Perk coffee shop employee, harbored unrequited feelings for Jennifer Aniston’s character, Rachel Green.

This resulted in a ten-year career that saw him appear in 148 of the 236 Friends episodes; he is currently the most frequently appearing recurrent character actor on the program.

2009 saw Tyler officially launch the temporary Central Perk pop-up replica in London in celebration of Friends’ 15th anniversary.

Fans could apply for tickets to see show memorabilia and receive a free coffee during its two-week run from September to October.

In the never-aired pilot of the satirical sitcom Nobody’s Watching, he played himself on the Central Perk set.

During Friends’ 20th anniversary in 2014, Tyler performed at a SoHo Central Perk parody.

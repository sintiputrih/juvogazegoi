What is Enes Yilmazer’s net worth?

Introduction

Enes Yilmazer is a Turkish-American entrepreneur, YouTuber, and real estate investor who has made a name for himself thanks to his popular videos where he tours some of the most expensive videos in the world.

He made a lot of his money flipping properties in Texas before moving to LA full time to pursue a career in real estate.

Yilmazer made content creation on YouTube his full-time job in 2018.

The Origin Story

Enes Yilmazer was born in Turkey in 1990 and lived in the country for seventeen years before moving to the United States.

He was born and raised in a small town on the Turkish coast that is known worldwide as one of the best windsurfing destinations on the planet.

He started windsurfing at the age of 12 and was crowned the World Youth Champion in 2009.

When he first arrived in the United States, he couldn’t speak a word of English, so his transition proved to be a steep learning curve.

Enes studied at Texas A&M University, Corpus Christi, between 2008 and 2012, where he received a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration and Management.

This set him up for a career in real estate, and after a few entry-level jobs, he was motivated to found his own company – Centrum Property Investment LLC in LA.

It’s clear that Enes loves what he does, and he is super enthusiastic about the stunning properties that he tours.

In a video that welcomes people to his channel, Enes said: “I’m a content creator and real estate enthusiast based out of Los Angeles. On this channel, we tour some of the most incredible properties in the country and around the world.”

He lived in Texas until 2018, when he sold his home and moved to LA full-time.

Real Estate

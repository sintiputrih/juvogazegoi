Venusaur

Venusaur (Japanese: フシギバナ Fushigibana) is a Grass-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Expedition Base Set.

Card text

e-Reader data

The card ID of the Holofoil and Box Topper prints is B-65. The card ID of the Non Holofoil print is B-65-#. On the Non Holofoil print, the short strip contains Pokédex information and a brief card summary. There is no long strip for this card. The species, height, and weight are present on the Holofoil and Box Topper prints.

Release information

This card was included as a Holo and Non Holo card in the Expedition Base Set, first released in the Base Expansion Pack in Japan. A Reverse Holo Jumbo print was also released as a Box Topper included in booster boxes of the English expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Body Slam is a move in the Pokémon games that Venusaur can learn, either via TM08 (in Generation I) or Move Tutor (in Generation III). This card's English e-Reader Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Crystal, whereas the Japanese entry comes from Pokémon Gold.

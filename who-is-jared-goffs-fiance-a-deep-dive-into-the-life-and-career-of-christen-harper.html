Who is Jared Goff's Fiance? A deep-dive into the life and career of Christen Harper

January 16, 2024
blog

Christen Harper, the fiancee of NFL quarterback Jared Goff, has been making headlines not only for her relationship with Goff but also for her own accomplishments in the modeling and acting industry. 

Early Life and Background

Christen Harper was born on May 28, 1993, to Michael and Daria Harper in Valencia, California.

She grew up alongside her older brother, Shane Harper, who is a professional ice hockey player with an impressive career in the American Hockey League and abroad. Christen attended California State University, Northridge, where she laid the foundation for her future career endeavors.

Modeling Career

Harper's journey into the modeling industry began at a young age. She decided to pursue modeling as a means to pay for her college education, and this decision ultimately led her to a successful career in the industry.

She signed with LA Models and went on to work with renowned brands such as GUESS and Third Love. Currently, she is associated with Wilhelmina in Miami and L.A., solidifying her status as an established model.

Her presence on social media has further amplified her popularity, with a significant following appreciating her professional photos and travel updates.

Acting Ventures

In addition to her modeling career, Christen Harper has ventured into the world of acting. She honed her acting skills at the Baron Brown studio and has since made notable appearances in the movie industry.

Her acting debut came with a guest star role in Entourage several years ago, and she further showcased her talent in the sci-fi movie Auggie and The Obituary of Tunde Johnson, both released in 2019. With her impressive performances and promising potential, Harper's future in the film industry looks bright.

Personal Life and Relationship with Jared Goff

Christen Harper's relationship with NFL quarterback Jared Goff has garnered significant attention. The couple first met in 2019 through the exclusive dating app, Raya. Their public debut as a couple was at the 2019 ESPYs, and they have since been inseparable, supporting each other through their respective professional endeavors.

Despite Goff's trade to the Detroit Lions, Harper made the move to Michigan with him, showcasing her commitment to their relationship. The couple got engaged in June 2022 during a trip to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, marking a significant milestone in their journey together.

Physical Attributes

Christen Harper's striking physical attributes have contributed to her success in the modeling industry. Standing at 5 feet 9 inches, or 176 cm, she possesses a captivating presence that turns heads wherever she goes. Her body measurements of 34-27-35 complement her graceful stature, further enhancing her appeal as a model.

 

Christen Harper has carved out a remarkable career for herself in the modeling and acting industry. From her early aspirations to pay for college through modeling to her flourishing career today, Harper's journey is a testament to her resilience and talent. Her relationship with Jared Goff has provided a glimpse into her personal life, showcasing her unwavering support for her fiance's career.

Through this deep-dive into her life and career, it is evident that Christen Harper is more than just Jared Goff's fiance – she is a multifaceted individual with a compelling story of her own.

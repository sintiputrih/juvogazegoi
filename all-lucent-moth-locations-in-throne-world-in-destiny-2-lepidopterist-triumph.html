All Lucent Moth locations in Throne World in Destiny 2 - Lepidopterist triumph

August 15, 2024
blog
Recommended Videos

You’ll find several mysteries awaiting you on Savathûn’s Throne World in Destiny 2’s The Witch Queen expansion. One of these mysteries are the Lucent Moths you can find hidden throughout the region. These Lucent Moths are collectibles that you’ll need to bring back to Fynch in the Quagmire. There are 10 of them on the Throne World region. In this guide, we cover all Lucent Moth locations on Throne World in Destiny 2 to complete the Lepidopterist triumph.

All Lucent Moth locations

Lucent Moth 1

This is a Lucent Moth we found in the Quagmire region. You can find it just before the Lost Sector in this region. If you’re the first to reach this location, you can typically find a Lightbearing Hive protecting it.

After you’re done, turn around, and you’ll find the Lucent Moth sitting on the interior, next to the wall you entered.

Lucent Moth 2

The second moth we’ve found is much deeper in Savathûn’s Throne World. You’ll need to make your way through the Quagmire to the southeast, into the Apothecary. In this region, our icon was no longer available on the map, but the location is roughly here, pictured below.

Then, you want to find a Deepsight location to reveal Savathûn ruins throughout the structure you’re standing on. Finally, shoot all of the runes to reveal the Lucent Moth.

Lucent Moth 3

For the third Lucent Moth, you’ll be able to find this one in the Alluring Curtain. You want to follow the same path from the previous moth, but look for a Deepsight right before you enter the Apothecary, in the second area, shortly after you’ve jumped over. You’ll head to the second level of the Alluring Curtain, and you’ll fight against a Moth Keeper. Upon defeating the Moth Keeper, you’ll find the third one.

Lucent Moth 4

The fourth Lucent Moth will be away from this location, and you can instead find it in the Temple of the Cunning. You can reach this location by going through the Quagmire’s Temple of the Warthful, and making your way through that area until you reach the orange portal. Go through it, and then continue immediately forward from this area into the Temple of the Cunning.

The path will contain multiple Scorn. You’ll want to continue going through this area, and take the pathway to the left. As you continue down this area, you’ll eventually receive a notification about entering the Temple of Cunning, showing you’re in the correct location. Continue through the area, and keep the left as much as possible, and you’ll find the Lucent Moth on top of a pillar with multiple Scorn in the same room.

Lucent Moth 5

The fifth Lucent Moth will be in the Temple of the Wrathful, the previous location you visited while searching for the fourth Lucent Moth. The path is mostly the same, however, instead of going forward when you make your way through the orange portal, turn to your right and fight the small squad of Scorn. Upon their defeat, you’ll see an orange bubble behind them pop, and there will be the Lucent Moth 5.

Lucent Moth 6

The sixth Lucent Moth will be in the Miasma region, which is to the north of Quagmire where we’ve grabbed the two previous Lucent Moths. Unlike the other ones, this one is a bit more out in the open. You can find it at the top of a tree in this area.

You can reach it by jumping on a rock behind the tree, and then reaching the branches to the right of the Moth.

Lucent Moth 7

The seventh Lucent moth will be in the same region as the previous one, in Miasma, but you’ll be searching for it in the Witch’s Echo. Proceed to that location, and then delve inside of it, but avoid using the orange portal to go further. Instead, reach the main chamber, and take a left.

As you progress through this pathway, there will be a room with three Scorn you can eliminate. There will also be a Deepsight for you to use. Interact with it, and the Lucent Moth will reveal itself behind the wall.

Lucent Moth 8

The eight Lucent Moth is a bit more tricky. To find it, you’ll need to begin the Wellspring: Defend mission on the Throne World. After starting it, proceed to the room to the left, and you’ll find the Lucent Moth being held by one of the statues in the next room.

Lucent Moth 9

For this Lucent Moth, you’ll need to visit the Altar of Reflection, which you can do so by clicking on the icon at the center of the map to make it the quickest trip. After that, follow the process through the Altar of Reflection as you normally would, and complete it. However, stop before the memory. In the room where you battle the multiple Hive enemies, on the right side, look for the Deepsight icon on the platform.

Interact with it, and then turn around. You want to reach the center platform to make it to the top and find the next Lucent Moth.

Lucent Moth 10

For this Lucent Moth, you won’t have to go too far to search for it. You’ll need to head to the center of Florescent Canal, on the east side of The Throne World Map. Once you reach there, search for a fountain. The Lucent Moth will be on top of a small disc circling the interior of the fountain.

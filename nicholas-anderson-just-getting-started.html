Early Beginnings and College Success

Despite being labeled as an undersized player, Anderson's determination and work ethic propelled him to excel in high school football and caught the attention of college recruiters.

After graduating from Vicksburg High School, Anderson continued his football journey at Jones County Junior College before transferring to Tulane University. Known for his natural instincts, anticipation, and exceptional football IQ, Anderson led the American Athletic Conference in tackles, playing a crucial role in Tulane's success.

A Shot with the New Orleans Saints

In May of 2023, Anderson received an opportunity to showcase his skills with the New Orleans Saints. He signed with the team as an undrafted free agent and participated in the Saints' rookie camp. Throughout the preseason, Anderson's talent and determination were evident as he made a significant impact on the field. Despite the fierce competition, he stood out with his explosive tackling, versatility, and relentless pursuit of the ball.

Although Anderson's journey with the Saints did not result in him making the opening day roster, he remained resilient and focused on his ultimate goal of becoming an NFL starter. His positive attitude, exceptional work ethic, and commitment to continuous improvement caught the attention of fans, coaches, and media alike.

Embracing the Opportunity

Staying Prepared for the Next Opportunity

Despite the challenges he has faced, Anderson remains optimistic and determined. He understands that in the world of professional sports, opportunities can arise at any moment.

Is This Why Cynthia Bailey Really Left 'RHOA'?

August 09, 2024
blog

Real Housewives Of Atlanta star Cynthia Bailey shocked her fans and the media when she announced, in an Instagram post, that she is leaving the Bravo series. Bailey has starred in the RHOA series since the 3rd season of the show in 2010.

The 54-year-old reality TV star and actress has also worked in modeling. Cynthia married Thomas in 2010 and divorced him in 2017. She’s been in a relationship with American sportscaster Mike Hill since 2018. Cynthia and Mike got married in October 2020. She has participated in several TV films and series, such as Black Friday, Cupcake Wars, Last Call, and Tales. Bailey also had several music video appearances and some big-screen movie roles.

What are the reasons that pushed Cynthia Bailey to leave the RHOA cast after starring for 11 consecutive seasons in the series? Let's find out.

10 Cynthia Is Happy But Also Sad That She Left 'RHOA'

In an interview with E! News Daily Pop, Cynthia revealed that she’s feeling emotions of happiness and sadness together, following her decision to quit The Real Housewives Of Atlanta series. She also questioned herself and was in disbelief that she starred in the show for 11 years. She added that she’s proud of herself and the brand image that she built.

9 She Revealed Her Decision To Leave Was Not Spontaneous

Cynthia Bailey said that it was time that she left the RHOA series. She revealed that she had planned for her exit and that her decision was not immediate. Cynthia thought about it and took her decision after reviewing her plans. She said she didn’t want to stay forever on the show.

She knew that it wouldn't last forever.

8 She Will Appear As A Friend In 'RHOA’s' 14th Season

In the upcoming 14th Season of The Real Housewives Of Atlanta, Cynthia Bailey declared that she was offered to participate in the series as a friend. She added that the friend contract was first introduced to the show by her. Cynthia liked the idea, which further motivated her to leave the show. She was given the authorization to appear later on at her own convenience.

7 Cynthia Bailey Denies That Her Husband Made Her Leave 'RHOA'

Cynthia thanked her LA-based husband Mike Hill for his support for her and shut off all claims that he is involved in her decision to leave The Real Housewives Of Atlanta’s cast. She said on Daily Pop that Mike wants the best for her and wants her to be happy. She added that he is supportive of the decisions she makes.

6 Cynthia Bailey Will Still Participate In 'The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip'

Actually, Cynthia didn’t totally leave The Real Housewives franchise. She will be participating later this year in The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip. The series will premiere on Peacock in November 2021.

Other cast members include Kyle Richards, Melissa Gorga, Luann De Lesseps, Kenya Moore, Teresa Giudice, and Ramona Singer.

5 She Has A Million Projects With Her Husband

While chatting with photographers at the Los Angeles international airport, Cynthia Bailey revealed to Page Six that she has a million projects that she will work on after she left RHOA. She added that she will work on those projects with her husband, Mike Hill.

She also said that the sky is the limit for the plans she has.

4 Cynthia Bailey Intends To Spend Some More Time In Los Angeles

Bailey was in Los Angeles for work. However, she intends to travel more to LA and connect with her husband, Mike Hill. This is what she revealed to Page Six at LA international airport. Cynthia got engaged to Fox Sports broadcaster Mike Hill in 2019, and the couple got married in October 2020.

3 She Will Focus Again On Her Eyewear Business

Since June 2020, Cynthia Bailey hasn’t been focusing on her eyewear business. However, the reality TV star took to Instagram, on September 30, to announce that the Cynthia Bailey Eyewear collection is coming back soon. She made the announcement while posing in eyewear of her own brand. Bailey also lately collaborated with Nila Bags, a business that sells women's handmade bags.

2 Cynthia Bailey Has A Net Worth Of $2.5 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Cynthia Bailey accumulated a wealth of $2.5 million. She did that during the time she spent on the RHOA show. She was getting paid $300,000 per season. Bailey will now move on to contemplate other business opportunities that will furthermore increase her wealth.

1 She May Still Change Her Mind

Cynthia Bailey revealed in an interview that she has good relations with NBC Original, Bravo, and Truly Original. The reality TV star said that she will return to The Real Housewives Of Atlanta if she misses her role or finds out later that her decision of leaving was not the right choice.

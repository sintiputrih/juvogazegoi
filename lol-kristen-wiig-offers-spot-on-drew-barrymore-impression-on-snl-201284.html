LOL! Kristen Wiig Offers Spot-On Drew Barrymore Impression on SNL

blog

Wonder what Fabrizio would say!

Saturday Night Live returned to NBC this weekend after a short hiatus, and series costar Kristen Wiig brought the laughs with another spot-on impression of Drew Barrymore in a skit that also featured “appearances” by Zooey Deschanel, Michael Cera and Fran Drescher.

Taking part in the latest installment of “Bein’ Quirky With Zooey Deschanel,” Wiig, as her Whip It costar Barrymore, chatted with Deschanel (Abby Elliott) about possible names for the baby she’s expecting with fiance Will Kopelman.

“If I had a boy, I’d name him Rolf, because it sounds like a fat dog laughing,” Wiig, as Barrymore, says in the clip, using Barrymore’s signature pauses in speech.

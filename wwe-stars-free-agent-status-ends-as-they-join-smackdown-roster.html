WWE Stars Free Agent Status Ends as They Join SmackDown Roster

August 10, 2024
blog

The status of Cedric Alexander within the company has undergone a significant change!

After initially being classified as a ‘free agent’ in the 2023 WWE Draft, which allowed him to appear on RAW, SmackDown, and NXT at will, Cedric Alexander has now officially joined the SmackDown brand, as noted on the official company website.

This transformation comes after Cedric Alexander made some notable appearances on SmackDown, where he participated in two intense matches against Dragon Lee. His impressive performances seem to have earned him a permanent spot on the SmackDown roster.

The WWE Draft saw several superstars labeled as ‘free agents,’ providing them with the flexibility to showcase their skills across different WWE brands. However, with his newfound home on SmackDown, Cedric Alexander is now a full-time member of the blue brand.

While Cedric Alexander has solidified his spot on SmackDown, there are still other WWE stars who remain free agents, including Randy Orton, Omos, Von Wagner, Xyon Quinn, Brock Lesnar, and United States Champion Logan Paul.

WWE fans can look forward to seeing more of Cedric Alexander’s exciting in-ring action on SmackDown as he embarks on this new chapter in his WWE journey!

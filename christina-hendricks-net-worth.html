Christina Hendricks’ Career Path

Christina Hendricks started her career as a model after winning a competition to appear on the cover of Seventeen magazine. She traveled the world as a successful model before transitioning into commercial work and relocating to Los Angeles. Hendricks started her acting career with roles in MTV’s Undressed and Showtime’s Beggars and Choosers while also making a guest appearance in Angel. Christina Hendricks’ Breakthrough and Career During “Mad Men” Years

Christina Hendricks’ career breakthrough came in 2007 when she landed a recurring role in the hit series “Mad Men.” She played Joan Halloway, an office manager who mentors other women at an advertising agency. The show ran until 2015 and Hendricks received critical acclaim for her performance, winning several awards and nominations.

During her time on “Mad Men,” Hendricks also appeared in other projects.
